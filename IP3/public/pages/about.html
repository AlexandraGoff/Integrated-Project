About us

This application was created to allow users to be able to track verious flights while also being able to see

what the weather conditions will be like during the flight's journey.

Users will be able to create an account and keep a log of all flights they have previously viewed.

Created by Alexandra Goffova, Maimoon Hashim, Sam Cooke, Julius Wolf, Sam Parks, Josh Reid and William Cann.
