About Us


Caledonian Air is a new state of the art flight-based weather application

Our application allows our users access to an array of different features


Our Weather section allows users the ability to physically set locations around an interactive world globe.

to reveal realtime info on Weather, Wind, humidity, temperature, danger warnings and more.


Our Flight section deals with current details on specific user flights

We also have the ability for our users to log previous flights, which are available to view at any time.


Web Application developed on by: Alexandra Goffova, Maimoon Mahmood, Sam Parks, Samuel Cooke, Josh Reid, William Cann and Julius Wolf.
